About Me

I am a dedicated academic and researcher with a passion for pushing the boundaries of knowledge in data science and machine learning. My journey spans from theoretical research to practical applications that make a real-world impact.

Currently serving as a Professor at Algonquin College, I bring extensive experience from my 8+ years as Process Lead at King Abdullah University of Science and Technology (KAUST), where I led groundbreaking research in advanced analytics and computational modeling.

My research focuses on developing innovative approaches to complex problems in agriculture, environmental modeling, and data assimilation, always with an eye toward sustainable solutions for global challenges.
